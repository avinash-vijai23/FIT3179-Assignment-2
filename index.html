TOP 1000 FILMS OF ALL TIME (1972-2019)

This data explores the highest grossing films at the box office from the year 1972 all the way to 2019. Film has been an important form of entertainment for many individuals and this data will be exploring the change of audiences tastes and recurring trends in regards to cast,crew and audience appeal of all the highest grossing films releases in this time frame. Note: This data does not include 2020-2021 re-releases or new releases.

Top Grossing Films of Each Decade

This visualisation explores the and compares the highest grossing films of each decade across a 5-decade period. It allows is to see the change in audience taste in terms of genre, the distribution and wide-releases of the movie increases from decade to decade.

Top Films of Each Year

Here we get a larger scope that shows all the data on a year-to-year basis, scrolling the selection parameter will allow you to explore the releases in each year, hovering over the visualisation will allow you to see attributes of the film that was released during the period. The importance of audience criticism and overall quality of the film has been reflected the IMDb rating which an industry standard used to measure the critical success of a film.
