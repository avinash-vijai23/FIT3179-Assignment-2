Top 1000 Films of All Time

A deeper look at the highest grossing films of all time and an analysis of the change in trends on and audience taste throughout time. The data will look at all films released between 1972 and 2019 as well as the casts and crews that were incolved with the production and distribution of films. Critical responses frome audiences will also be assessed and evaluated in the form of IMDb ratings.

The main dataset can be found from Kaggle

Top Films of Each Decade (1970s- 2010s)

This visualisation is a small time lapse that shows the change in tastes and trends of the worldwide film-going audience

Star Wars is considered the first modern blockbuster. It spawned a billion dollar franchise that went on to break many box office of record. The films comes under the Sci-Fi Fantasy genre and was one of the earliest movies of the genre to have a huge culture impact

E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial was another film with a huge culture impact, directed by Steven Speilberg. It was a Sci-fi Coming-of-age drama which represents a period where films based around children had huge box office success. Following Jaws this was one of the films that solidified Speilberg's influence in the industry.

Titanic was a radical departure from the previous films which were based around a Sci-fi plot, this film was a historical-disaster film directed by James Cameron which centered around a young romance. The film went on to win Best Picture at Academy Awards and also proved that critically acclaimed films can also be successful at the box office.

Another film by James Cameron, Avatar is seen as a huge technincal achievement in filmmaking with regards to its use of CGI. It once again signalled the return of the dominance of Sci-fi films at the world box office.

The 2010s saw the dominance of superhero comic-book based films. Avengers: Endgame represented the culmination of 10 years worth of character development and is the first sequel to make an appearance of the list of the highest grossing films in each decade. It is also worth noting that over the course of 10 years the Marvel films have outgrossed 60 years worth of James Bond films and 50 years worth of Star Wars films which is seen as a huge acheivement.
